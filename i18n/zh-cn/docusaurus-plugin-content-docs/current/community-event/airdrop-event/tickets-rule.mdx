#### New Available <Highlight color="#25c2a0">Devnet</Highlight>

### Vote for Podcast & Support Podcast

1. If you complete **Vote for Podcast & Support Podcast**, you will get **1 ticket**.

### Circlepod Promoter

1. If you have already joined in **Vote for Podcast & Support Podcast**:
    1. Enter the official invitation code to get **1 ticket**.
    2. Every time you invite 1 friend and help your friend complete the **Vote for Podcast & Support Podcast**, you will get **1 ticket**.
2. If you are a `new friend`:
    1. Enter your friend's invitation code to get **1 ticket**.
    2. After completing **Vote for Podcast & Support Podcast**, you can get 1 ticket.
    3. After completing the event, new friends can refer to the tips above to get more tickets!

P.S. More information will be released before the event!

### Claim your Rewards

1. If you complete the **Undisclosed Event**, you will get **1 ticket**.
