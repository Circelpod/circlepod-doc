## What is Channel?

The channel is a kind of Stake Pool(farm), and audience can stack CPX through their support to increase the energy value. The certified Podcaster can get the energy dividend stored in the program.

## Channel Support

[Channel Member](/docs/channel/channel-member) can support channel and stake [CPX](/docs/tokenomics/intro).

Circlepod will drop Rewards based on [Tokenomics](/docs/tokenomics/intro) and irregular activities
