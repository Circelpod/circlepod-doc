## What is Channel Member?

**Channel Member** is an operational account of Channel Pool. A Channel corresponds to a Channel Member.
Before being able to stake the channel pool, one must create a **Channel Member** account with the Channel.
Each member has four types of token vaults making up balances of a channel member:

* Stake pool token: The total amount of pool tokens created from staking `(stake = stake-pool-token * stake-pool-token-price)`.
* Stake Vault: the total amount of tokens staked.
* Vault: Available balances is a zero-interest earning token account with no restrictions.
* Vault Pending Withdrawal Balance: Unstaked funds incurs an unbonding timelock after 7 days.
