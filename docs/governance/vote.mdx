## Vote for your loved podcaster
:::caution Please check these things below:
- Your Circlepod wallet is connected! More details: [Click](/docs/wallet/connect-wallet)
- You have enough CPX to vote! More details: [Click](/docs/wallet/airdrop)
:::

### Step 1. Choose your loved podcast
![Apply to Airdrops](/img/event/vote/choose-to-vote.png)

### Step 2. Enter your votes that you want to vote
If the podcast have 30 votes, the channel pool will be generated. After that you can support for that Podcast!
![Apply to Airdrops](/img/event/vote/ticket.png)
:::caution
- You can only vote each podcast for one time.
:::

### Step 3. Vote for Podcast successfully!
![Apply to Airdrops](/img/event/vote/vote-successfully.png)

:::tip
- The number of total votes is not real time. We update the total votes every 30 minutes! 
:::
