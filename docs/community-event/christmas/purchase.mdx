# Purchase plan

## NFT
If you purchase in Circlepod store, you can choose and get different [NFTs](/docs/community-event/christmas/nft) when spending the specified amount.(3 styles in total)

- one leaf 🌱： Get 1 NFT with a purchase of over $59.
- Pile of leaves 🌱🌱: Get 2 NFTs with a purchase of over $108.
- Bag of leaves 🌱🌱🌱： Get 3 NFTs with a purchase of over $159.

:::caution
❗️The amount mentioned above is the total amount excluding shipping❗️
:::

## Special Role - Circlepod Shoper
❗️As long as you meet any of the above plans, you can get **Special Role** in Discord❗️

- We will give priority to the discount information of the market in this group.
- Participate in the discussion of physical product opinions.


## Invitation Code
As long as you meet any of the above plans, you can get **Invitation code** of App, and you can unlock the core functions of the Circlepod App.
