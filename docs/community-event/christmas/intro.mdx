## Purchase plan

If you purchase in Circlepod store, you can choose and get different NFTs when spending the specified amount.(3 styles in total)

- one leaf 🌱： Get 1 NFT with a purchase of over $59.
- Pile of leaves 🌱🌱: Get 2 NFTs with a purchase of over $108.
- Bag of leaves 🌱🌱🌱： Get 3 NFTs with a purchase of over $159.

❗️As long as you meet any of the above plans, you can get **Special Role** in Discord & **Invitation code** of App❗️

❗️The amount mentioned above is the total amount excluding shipping❗️
