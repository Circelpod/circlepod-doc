# Channel

## What is Channel

The channel is a kind of Stake Pool, and listeners can stack CPX through their support to increase the energy value. The certified Podcaster can get the energy dividend stored in the program.

## Channel Support

[Channel Member](/docs/channel-member) can support channel and stake [CPX](/docs/tokenomics).

Circlepod will drop Rewards based on [Tokenomics](/docs/tokenomics) and irregular activities
